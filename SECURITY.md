# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Email **security@milton.co**. For internal reports you may also DM Chinedum on Slack.

Please **do not** open a public GitHub issue for security reports.

Include:

- What you found
- Steps to reproduce
- Affected repo or service
- Your assessment of impact

We'll acknowledge within **48 hours** and follow up with remediation status.

## Scope

This policy covers all repositories under the `Milton-Group` GitHub organization and services hosted at `*.milton.co`.

## Out of scope

- Denial-of-service testing against production services
- Social engineering of Milton staff
- Physical security
